    Work Experience

    My career in the IT industry began in 1991 when I completed the 30-week "Computer Career Program" at Maryville University in St. Louis, MO. This program was an acclerated program designed to jump start a career in IT.

    My first IT job was as a COBOL developer for a very small firm (less than 10 employees) and from there I moved to the corporate world with MCI Communications. Moving on to Bunge North America I began developing user interfaces using the PowerBuilder development tool. In 1996, because of my PowerBuilder experience, I was hired by AT&T (Southwestern Bell at the time) where I spent 23 years, first as a developer and the last 15 years as a technical solutions architect.
